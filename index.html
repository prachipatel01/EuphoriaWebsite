Tutorial

How to use?

Connect the device with the App.

Turn on bluetooth from your phone and open the App. Switch on the Device and the App will detect your device.

Make sure all the cartridges are present.

Insert all the cartridges into the device before connecting with the App. To create your favourite shade the device requires all the cartridges.

Select your favourite shade from the color pallet.

We have all the shades starting from tints of red to tints of pink, brown and many more. Just select which ever you like. It's all yours.

See how it looks on your face.

You have the option to see how the selected shade looks on your face. Just open the camera in the App and have a click. Smile Please!

Glad you like the shade! Now just press CREATE SHADE

on clicking CREATE SHADE it will create your shade from the device. Wait for few seconds please!

Apply the shade. Damn, you look gorgeous!

Take the brush applicator and mix the shade before applying. It's that simple.
